About us

Apollo Bay Welding and Fabrication was started by Mark Nipper in early 2022 off the back of a move from Melbourne with his family. With over 20 years experience, Mark brings his unique expertise to Apollo Bay and its surrounds. Mark provides a high level of professionalism and customer satisfaction to all his projects, large or small, and takes pride in his quality of work and service, all within a timely manner. His reputation and ability to provide a personalised service ensures he can tailor a solution to ensure you get the results you want.
